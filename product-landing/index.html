Alarms

Register here,

Alarms are used to wake people up in the morning. It works by triggering a mechanism which enables them to sound a loud noise that would only be disabled by a human pressing some button (or in some digital ones, alarms disables or lowers their volumes with the help of a sensor). People both need and hates alarms. They do wake us up very efficiently, but it results on us waking up suddenly. This leads to people not wanting to use their alarms even when they really need them.
